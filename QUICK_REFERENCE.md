## ğŸ“ Common Questions

### Q: How do I share my data with my doctor?
**A**: Generate a report, then print or save as PDF to share.

### Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?
**A**: No, data is stored locally. Export from one device and import to another.

### Q: What if I clear my browser data?
**A**: All data will be lost. Always keep backups via export feature!

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A**: Data never leaves your device, but it's not encrypted at rest.

### Q: Can I recover deleted entries?
**A**: No, deletions are permanent. Export regularly to create backups.

### Q: How do I change my weight goal?

### Q: What if I miss logging a medication?
