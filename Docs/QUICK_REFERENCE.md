## 🔄 Managing Sample Data

### Load Sample Data
1. Click purple button (top-right)
2. Click "Load Sample Data"
3. Refresh if needed

### Clear Sample Data
1. Click purple button again
2. Click "Clear Sample Data"
3. Confirm deletion

### Export Sample Data
1. Navigate to Settings
2. Click "Export Data"
3. Choose format (JSON/CSV)
