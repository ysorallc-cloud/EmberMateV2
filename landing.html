Frequently Asked Questions

Is EmberMate HIPAA compliant?

No, EmberMate is a demonstration application and is NOT HIPAA compliant. Do not use it for real personal health information. It's designed for educational purposes only.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser using LocalStorage. Nothing is sent to any server or cloud service.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required! Just launch the app and start using it immediately.

Does it work offline?

Yes, once loaded, the app works completely offline. You only need an internet connection for the initial load.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your health data and reports from the Analytics section.

What browsers are supported?

EmberMate works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
