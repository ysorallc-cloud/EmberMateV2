🔒 Privacy Policy & HIPAA Compliance

Data Storage & Encryption

EmberMate stores all your health information locally on your device using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

HIPAA Compliance

This application implements technical safeguards required by HIPAA including:

  • Data encryption at rest using AES-256
  • Audit logging of all data access and modifications
  • Automatic session timeout after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Secure data export with encryption

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data at any time
  • Export: Download your data in encrypted format
  • Delete: Clear all data from your device
  • Modify: Edit or update any information

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We recommend regular encrypted backups for data safety.

📜 Audit Log

All data access and modifications are logged for security and compliance purposes.
